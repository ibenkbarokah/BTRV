What is BTR?

NFT marketplace platform for Collectibles & luxury goods

BTR is a model that links luxury products with NFT and provides a marketplace that enables auction, sales, and lucky draw.(with donation)

BTR Token used for bidding purchase and subscription will be burned and therefore excluded from the distribution amount.
And the collected BTR Tokens will be donated to designated organizations except for the minimum operation cost.
